Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our excavation and demolition services in the Grand Rapids area.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all our excavation and demolition services. We'll visit your property, assess the project, and provide a detailed quote with transparent pricing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Affordable Excavating Inc. is fully licensed, bonded, and insured to operate in Michigan. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation to protect both our team and your property.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small residential projects like pool removals typically take 2-4 days, while larger commercial excavations may take several weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our estimate and work efficiently to complete projects on schedule.

Do you handle permits and regulations?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and ensure compliance with local regulations and building codes. We have extensive experience navigating Grand Rapids and West Michigan permitting processes to make your project hassle-free.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Grand Rapids and all of West Michigan, including Holland, Muskegon, Kalamazoo, Battle Creek, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.
